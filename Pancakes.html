Pancakes

Ingredients

1 cup Self-raising flour, sifted
1 tbsp sugar
1 egg (lightly beaten)
3/4 cup milk
50 g butter (melted)
Syrup
Cinnamon

Equipment

Whisk
Large pan
Bowls
Measuring set

Method

1. Gather all ingredients.
2. Grab a bowl and put in the flour, sugar and cinnamon and whisk.
3. Pour in the egg and whisk. Then pour in the milk little by little and continue to whisk until it is smooth and lump free. You don’t want your batter to be super thick, but you also don’t want it to be super runny either.
4.This is an extra step for people who have a sweet tooth. You can add a bit of syrup to your batter.
5.In a hot pan or flat grill over medium heat, brush butter over the cooking surface and pour 1/4 cup measures for each pancake.
6.When large bubbles form on the surface, flip the pancake over and cook until lightly golden on the other side.
